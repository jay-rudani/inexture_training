-- super as a keyword :
-- super keyword is used to refer superclass or parent class of the current class
-- it is often used to access members of the superclass when they are overridden or hidden by the subclass

-- super as a constructor invocation :
-- in a subclass constructor, you can use the super keyword to explicitly call a constructor from the superclass
-- this is used when you want to perform some initialization that is common to both the subclass and the superclass
